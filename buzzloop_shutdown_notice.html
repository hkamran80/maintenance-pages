Shutdown Notice for Buzzloop

We are sorry to say that, as of July 22, 2020, Buzzloop for School Loop will be shutting down. This decision comes on the heels of the Acalanes Union High School District moving from School Loop to Canvas.

Buzzloop will be recreated from the ground-up to bring a new name, a fresh design, and a faster and more efficient underlying codebase.

We apologize for any inconviences this may have caused.

Thank you for using Buzzloop.

— The Buzzloop Team
